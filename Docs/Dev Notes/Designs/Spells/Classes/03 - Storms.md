# Class Identity
Crowd controller with respectable aggression at close to medium range. Likes to be up close and personal, but not quite as much as Stone.
## Passives
### Concussive Strikes
Weapon attacks deal increased damage against stunned targets. This damage scales per stack of Concussive Strikes. A stack is granted each time an enemy is stunned.
## Offensive Spells
### Buffet
**Quick spell** Blast a small cone of extreme winds in front of you, knocking targets back and dealing medium stun.
### Lightning Bolt
Fire a lightning bolt straight ahead, penetrates targets. High damage and high stun, with a high cooldown.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Lightning Strike: Hold cast instead of clicking to target a Lightning Strike from the sky with a radius of effect instead of a straight line bolt.
### Chain Lightning
Medium damage medium stun, chains within a certain distance. Deals less damage and stun per chain.
## Defensive / Utility Spells
### Thunderous Roar
Cause a calamitous crash of thunder affecting all enemies near your point of focus (alternate option; self cast) for little to no damage but very high stun
### Funnel Cloud
Spawn a funnel cloud which meanders around an area for a time. Any creatures hit by this cloud are launched into the air, interrupting whatever they might have been doing.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Ion Storm: Funnel Cloud becomes electric, dealing stun status to anything it hits
- Storm Warning: Funnel Cloud multiplies
