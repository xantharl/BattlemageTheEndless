# Class Identity
Aggressive hit and run tactics with limited self sustain, meant to encourage spreading as much fire as possible.
## Class Upgrades
- Base Damage
- Status Damage
- Duration
- Unstable Flames: Much more increase to fire damage with a chance of self damage
## Passives
### Raging Inferno
Gain stacks of raging inferno any time a Burning enemy dies. For each stack of raging inferno, any fire effect deals more damage or lasts longer per tick. This applies to combined statuses.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Inferno's Embrace: Gain fire resistance per stack of raging inferno. This can exceed 100% and stacks with other buffs/items.
- Gambler's Inferno: For each stack of raging inferno, gain a percent chance to double proc burning when applying a stack 
## Offensive Spells
### Fire Bolt
**Quick spell** You know it, you love it. It lights something on fire from a distance. Fairly low damage and kind of slow but reliable.
#### Upgrade Paths
- More projectiles / Shotgun style
- Faster projectiles
- Homing
### Fireball
You also know this one, and love it even more. A devastating fire AoE with an equally devastating cooldown. Has a large and fairly slow projectile which detonates on impact.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Larger Radius
- Faster projectile
- Secondary explosion(s)
#### Cast Sequence
Up, up, right
### Molten Nova
Erupt in a ring of fire, damaging and igniting anything in range
#### Upgrade Paths
- More/Faster projectiles
- Sticks around for a bit 
## Defensive / Utility Spells
### Nourishing Flames
Consume all stacks of Raging Inferno to heal a portion of your health per stack. Scales such that more stacks give more/stack.
### Cauterize
Stop bleeding and remove a debuff for a small amount of self damage.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Reduced damage taken
- Apply debuffs to a target
- Enable casting on allies
