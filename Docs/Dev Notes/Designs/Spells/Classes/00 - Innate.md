## Spell Scrolls
Allows finding and casting of spells not in the players classes. Requires preparation time scaling with spell strength.
### Enchant Weapon
Enchant your weapon with energy from the class of the spell cast most recently, causing it to deal increased damage and the appropriate status. The balance of damage and status varies per spell class. Has element specific upgrade paths.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Persistence: Enchant weapon no longer needs to be activated and is always on (when not on cooldown).
### Elemental Shield
Surround yourself with elemental energy from the class of the spell cast most recently. Effects vary based on the active element. Has element specific upgrade paths.
#### Upgrade Paths
- Persistence: Elemental Shield no longer needs to be activated and is always on (when not on cooldown).
### Grapple
Grapple to a point, it's a grappling hook but magic.
