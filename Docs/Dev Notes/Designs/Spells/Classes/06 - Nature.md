# Class Identity
Battlefield manipulating healer with minor offense capability, damages through infection.
## Passives
### Airborne
On death, stacks of infection have a % chance to spread to nearby targets. 
#### Upgrade Paths
- Infectious Disease: increased chance to spread
- Open Wounds: Airborne can also spread bleed
- Contagion: All stacks of negative effects have a chance to spread
## Offensive Spells
### Nature's Grasp
**Quick spell** Ensnare a target, causing them to be temporarily immobile. Deals light damage and light infection.
### Thorns
Fire a burst of thorny projectiles with light homing. Deals light damage and infection per thorn.
### Noxious Spore
Lob a Noxious Spore. It explodes on impact, dealing light damage to its target and medium infection to anything in the explosion radius.
## Defensive / Utility Spells
### Briar Patch
Summon a field of thorny briar plant life centered on your aim. This area applies heavy infection to targets moving within.
### Harness Decay
Consume stacks of infection from the nearest infected target to heal yourself or an ally for an amount per stack.
#### Upgrade Paths:
- Kickbacks: Heal yourself for 50% of the target's healed amount when cast on an ally
