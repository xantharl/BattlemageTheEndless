## Sword
Light ground combo: Three back to back quick slashes
Heavy ground combo:
1. Spin
2. Knock back
3. Stabbing charge

Sprinting Light Combo:
1. Lunging slash
2. Upward slice which brings the player but not the enemy
3. Option to:
	1. Dodge forward and resume prior velocity
	2. Launch a sword arc at the crosshair

Sprinting Heavy Combo:
1. Drag sword, on release swings and knocks enemy up
2. Uppercut to meet enemy
3. Overhead slam back down

Falling light:
1. Lunging slash

Falling heavy:
1. Downward stab
